Welcome to Shenanigans!

Welcome to Shenanigans, the best country dance hall in Idaho! Here we provide great music, a pool hall, a mocktail bar and even dance lessons! We will be providing free instruction from 7-8 every night followed by dancing and everything else until 11 pm on the week nights and 1230 on the weekends. Be sure to follow us on Instagram at "shenanigansposse" for more exiting events and updates!

What is country dacning?

Country dancing is a combination of swing dancing and line dancing, all do country music! It is a great activity for kids, teenagers, college students, and even adults! It is a great way to spend your weekend!

About us

We at shenanigans love country dancing and wanted to expand it and share it with the community. We made it out job to bring more family fun activities to the community and we believe shenanigans does that perfectly. We have put blood, sweat, and tears into making it into what it is today. We have had many hardships but in the end it is all worth it.
